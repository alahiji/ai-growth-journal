## Emergency Burnout Protocol

**If you notice 3+ of these signs, IMMEDIATELY implement this protocol:**

### Signs:
- Persistent fatigue despite adequate sleep
- Declining interest in previously enjoyed activities
- Feeling guilty when not working/learning
- Physical symptoms (headaches, stomach issues)
- Irritability or mood changes
- Avoiding social connections
- Thoughts of "I can't do this" or "I'm not good enough"

### Protocol:
1. **STOP**: Cancel all non-essential commitments for 1 week
2. **REST**: Prioritize sleep, exercise, social connection
3. **REFLECT**: Journal about what led to this state
4. **REACH OUT**: Talk to mentor, therapist, or trusted friend
5. **RESET**: Before resuming, reduce goals by 50%
6. **MONITOR**: Daily check-ins for 2 weeks

**Remember**: You've been here before. You know the pattern. Don't let it complete the cycle this time.
