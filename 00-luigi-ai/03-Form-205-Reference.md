### Article 1 — Entity Name

```
Luigi AI LLC
```

- The name **must** include "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company" or an abbreviation
- ✅ "Luigi AI LLC" satisfies this requirement

---

### Article 2 — Registered Agent and Registered Office

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Registered Agent Type** | Individual (not an organization) |
| **Registered Agent Name** | [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] |

⚠️ **Critical Rules**:
- Must be a **physical street address** in Texas (no P.O. Box)
- The registered agent must have **consented** (see `02-Registered-Agent-Consent.md`)

---

### Article 3 — Governing Authority

**Question**: "Will the LLC be governed by managers?"

| If You Choose... | Enter... |
|-------------------|----------|
| **No** (member-managed) ← **Recommended** | Initial member name and address |
| Yes (manager-managed) | Initial manager name and address |

**For single-member, member-managed LLC:**

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Governing Authority Type** | Members |
| **Member Name** | [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME] |
| **Member Address** | [YOUR ADDRESS] |

> **Why member-managed?** As a single-member LLC, you are the only member and you manage the company. There's no need for a separate manager structure.

---

### Article 4 — Purpose

Enter the general purpose clause:

```
The purpose for which the company is formed is the transaction of any and all 
lawful business for which limited liability companies may be organized under 
the Texas Business Organizations Code.
```

> Do NOT limit yourself to "AI consulting" or any specific business type. The general clause lets you do anything legal.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | How to Avoid |
|---------|--------------|
| Using a P.O. Box for registered agent | Use your home/physical address |
| Forgetting "LLC" in the name | Always include "LLC" at the end |
| Choosing manager-managed unnecessarily | Single-member → member-managed |
| Specifying a narrow business purpose | Use the general purpose clause |
| Not checking name availability first | Search on SOSDirect before filing |
| Entering a future effective date accidentally | Select "on filing" unless you need a delay |
